Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 7, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Invio ("the app", "we", "us") handles information when you use our mobile application.

1. Overview

Invio is designed to work primarily offline. Your business profile, client details, and invoices are stored locally on your own device, not on our servers. We do not require account creation to use the app.

2. Information We Collect

Invio stores the following information locally on your device only:

  • Your business profile (business name, email, phone, address, logo, currency preference)
  • Client information you enter (name, company, email, phone, address)
  • Invoice and quote data you create (line items, amounts, notes, payment details)

None of this data is transmitted to us, sold, or shared with third parties. It exists only on your device unless you choose to share a generated PDF invoice yourself (e.g. via WhatsApp, email, or another app you select).

3. Subscription and Payment Information

If you choose to upgrade to Invio Pro, payment is processed through the Google Play Store billing system. We do not receive or store your payment card details — this is handled entirely by Google's payment infrastructure under their own privacy policy.

4. Data Storage and Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard secure local storage. Since data is not transmitted to external servers, it is not accessible to us. If you uninstall the app or clear its storage, this data is permanently deleted and cannot be recovered by us, as we do not hold a copy.

5. Permissions

Invio may request the following device permissions:

  • Storage/file access: to generate and save invoice PDFs
  • Sharing: to let you send invoices via other apps (WhatsApp, email, etc.) already installed on your device
  • Photo library access: to let you add a business logo
  • Notifications: to remind you when an invoice is due

6. Children's Privacy

Invio is not directed at children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect information from children.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the app after changes means you accept the updated policy.
