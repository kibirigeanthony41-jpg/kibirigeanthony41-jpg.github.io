Privacy Policy · Last updated: June 29, 2026

FocusLoop ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our mobile application FocusLoop (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

FocusLoop is designed with privacy in mind. We collect minimal data:

  • Usage Data: Focus session durations, habit completions, XP, streaks, and stats. This data is stored locally on your device only.
  • Settings: Your in-app preferences (session time, notifications, etc.) stored locally on your device.
  • No Account Required: FocusLoop does not require you to create an account or provide personal information.

2. How We Use Your Information

All data collected by FocusLoop is used solely to provide app functionality:

  • To track your focus sessions and habits
  • To display your progress, streaks, and XP
  • To save your preferences and settings

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties.

3. Data Storage

All your data is stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not transmit your data to any external servers. If you uninstall the app, all your data is permanently deleted.

4. Advertising

The free version of FocusLoop may display advertisements served by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain device information to serve relevant ads. Please refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details on how AdMob handles data.

5. Children's Privacy

FocusLoop is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
